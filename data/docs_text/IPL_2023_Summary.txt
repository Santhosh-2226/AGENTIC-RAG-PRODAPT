format after a four-year break caused by pandemic-era venue restrictions. that made the tournament feel more like the older ipl and reconnected franchises with their home crowds. Chennai Super Kings won the title by beating Gujarat Titans by five wickets in the final using the dls method. the result gave chennai a fifth ipl title. drawing level with Mumbai Indians. tournament structure and 
venues the league retained the 10-team. 74-match structure from 2022, again using two groups of five teams for scheduling. each side played 14 league matches before the playoffs. the 2023 season also introduced the impact player rule and expanded use of drs to wides and no-balls. those were not minor tweaks; they changed match management and selection logic. team / stage primary city venue capacity Chennai Super Kings chennai m. a. chidambaram stadium 38.000 Delhi Capitals delhi arun jaitley stadium 35.200 Gujarat Titans ahmedabad narendra modi stadium 132,000 Kolkata Knight Riders kolkata eden gardens 68.000 oun super lucknow brsabv ekana stadium 50.000 Mumbai Indians mumbai wankhede stadium 33.108 Punjab Kings mohali / dharamshala is bindra stadium / hpca stadium 27.000 / 23.000 Rajasthan Royals jaipur / guwahati sawai mansingh / aca stadium 30.000 / 46.000 beegaioe bengaluru m. chinnaswamy stadium 40.000 sunnsers hyderabad hyderabad rajiv gandhi intemational stadium 55.000 participating teams team head coach captain Chennai Super Kings stephen fleming MS Dhoni Delhi Capitals ricky ponting david warner
